## PRODUCTION NOTES

### ANIMATION APPROACH
**Option A: Stop-Motion Lego**
- Actual Lego bricks and minifigures
- Stop-motion frame-by-frame animation
- Most authentic Lego feel
- Higher production cost
- Slower production timeline

**Option B: CG Lego Simulation**
- Lego in computer-generated environment
- Maintains Lego aesthetic
- Faster production
- Lower cost than stop-motion
- Tools: Cinema 4D, Blender (with Lego-style materials)

**Option C: Hybrid Approach**
- Real Lego for some shots
- CG Lego for complex animations
- Best of both worlds
- Moderate cost

**Recommendation:** Option B (CG Lego) for 15-second videos—balances authenticity, speed, and cost
